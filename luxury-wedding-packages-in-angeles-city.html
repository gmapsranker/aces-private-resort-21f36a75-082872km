Frequently Asked Questions About Luxury Wedding Packages

What is included in your wedding packages and what are the starting prices?
Our luxury wedding packages at Aces Private Resort start at ₱150,000 for our Essential Package, which includes 6-hour venue rental, basic styling, catering for 100 guests, and a bridal suite. Our Premium Package (₱250,000) adds premium flower arrangements, a professional photo/video team, and accommodation for 20 guests. Our Exclusive Package (₱350,000) includes all premium features plus extended venue hours, complete styling, and accommodation for up to 40 guests. All packages include access to our infinity pool and garden areas for photos. Custom packages are also available to suit your specific needs.
How far in advance should we book for a wedding at Aces Private Resort?
We recommend booking at least 8-12 months in advance, especially for peak season dates (December to February and June to September). Weekend dates are particularly in demand. However, we do occasionally have available dates for shorter notice bookings during off-peak months. A 30% non-refundable deposit is required to secure your date, with the balance due 30 days before the event.
Can you accommodate outdoor ceremonies during rainy season in Pampanga?
Yes, we have contingency plans for wet weather. While our garden venue is perfect for dry season ceremonies (December to May), we provide a beautiful covered pavilion area as a backup during rainy season (June to November). The pavilion can accommodate up to 200 guests and offers stunning views of our landscaped gardens. We make the final call on venue setup 5 hours before the ceremony based on weather conditions, at no additional cost.
Do you have preferred vendors, or can we bring our own suppliers?
While we have a list of trusted local vendors in Angeles City and Pampanga who are familiar with our venue, you're welcome to bring your own suppliers. However, catering must be arranged through our in-house team or our three accredited caterers due to kitchen facility requirements. Outside suppliers must provide proper business permits and insurance, and attend a venue orientation at least one month before the event.
What accommodation options are available for wedding guests?
Our resort features 15 luxury rooms that can accommodate up to 45 guests. We offer special wedding rates for room blocks, with prices starting at ₱5,500 per night including breakfast. For larger groups, we have partnerships with nearby hotels in Angeles City (5-10 minutes away) that offer preferential rates. We can also arrange shuttle services between these hotels and our resort. Early check-in and late check-out can be arranged subject to availability.
