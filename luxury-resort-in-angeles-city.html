Frequently Asked Questions About Luxury Resort

What are your current room rates and what's included in the stay?
Our luxury villa rates start at ₱15,000 per night for our Classic Pool Villa (good for 4-6 persons) and go up to ₱25,000 for our Grand Villa (good for 8-10 persons). All stays include access to your private pool, complete kitchen facilities, free WiFi, welcome drinks, and complimentary breakfast for all guests. We also provide 24/7 butler service and free secure parking. Additional beds can be arranged for ₱1,500 per night.
How far are you from Clark International Airport and do you offer airport transfers?
We're located just 15 minutes from Clark International Airport in Angeles City, Pampanga. We offer premium airport transfers in our luxury van for ₱1,500 one-way or ₱2,500 round-trip. Please provide your flight details at least 24 hours before arrival so we can arrange your pickup. Our driver will be waiting at the arrival area with a name placard.
Do you allow events like intimate weddings or birthday celebrations?
Yes, we specialize in intimate events for up to 50 guests. Event packages start at ₱75,000 which includes venue rental, basic styling, catering for 30 persons, and overnight stay in one of our villas. We require a 50% deposit to secure your date. Our events team can help customize packages based on your specific needs and guest count. Please note that events must end by 10 PM due to local noise regulations.
What is your cancellation and rebooking policy?
For regular bookings, we offer free cancellation up to 7 days before check-in with full refund. Cancellations within 3-7 days will incur a 50% charge, while cancellations less than 3 days before check-in or no-shows will be charged in full. For event bookings, cancellations must be made 30 days prior for a full refund less 10% processing fee. Rebooking is allowed once within 6 months of original date, subject to availability.
Are there restaurants or activities nearby?
We're conveniently located 5 minutes from SM Clark and Clark Freeport Zone where you'll find numerous restaurants, shops, and entertainment options. Popular nearby attractions include Aqua Planet (10 mins), Nayong Pilipino (15 mins), and several golf courses. Our concierge can arrange restaurant reservations or activities like spa services, golf tee times, and tours of local attractions. We also have partnerships with several high-end restaurants that offer in-villa dining services.
