Frequently Asked Questions About Adventure and Outdoor Activities

What are your current room rates and what's included in the overnight stay?
Our room rates start at ₱3,500 per night for our Deluxe Room (good for 2), ₱5,500 for our Family Suite (good for 4), and ₱8,500 for our Presidential Suite (good for 6). All overnight stays include complimentary breakfast, access to our infinity pool, use of karaoke facilities until 10 PM, and free parking. For weekend bookings, we require a minimum 2-night stay during peak season (December-May).
How far are you from Manila and what's the best way to get there?
We're located in Apalit, Pampanga, approximately 62 kilometers from Manila. By car, it typically takes 1.5-2 hours via NLEX, taking the Apalit exit. We provide free parking for guests. For those taking public transport, you can take a bus to San Fernando, Pampanga, then take a tricycle to our resort. We also offer private shuttle services from Manila for ₱3,500 per way (good for up to 6 persons) - please book at least 48 hours in advance.
What adventure activities do you offer and do I need to book them in advance?
We offer several adventure activities including ATV rides through our nature trails (₱1,500/hour), zipline experiences (₱500/ride), wall climbing (₱300/session), and guided hiking tours (₱1,000/person). Advanced booking is required for all activities, ideally at least 24 hours before. Each activity includes safety equipment and a brief orientation with our certified instructors. Note that some activities may be weather-dependent.
What is your cancellation and refund policy?
For room bookings, we offer a full refund if cancelled 7 days before check-in date. Cancellations made 3-6 days prior receive a 50% refund. Cancellations within 48 hours of check-in are non-refundable. For activity bookings, we offer full refunds if cancelled 24 hours before the scheduled time, or if we need to cancel due to weather conditions. All refunds are processed within 7-10 business days.
Do you have restaurant facilities and can you accommodate special dietary requirements?
Yes, our in-house restaurant 'Ace Dining' is open daily from 6 AM to 10 PM, serving local Kapampangan specialties and international cuisine. We can accommodate vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free diets with advance notice. For special dietary requirements or allergies, please inform us at least 24 hours before your arrival. We also offer private dining experiences and romantic dinner setups by the pool with 48 hours advance booking (starting at ₱2,500 per couple).
