Frequently Asked Questions About Corporate Retreats and Team Building

What is your minimum group size for corporate retreats and what are the rates?
Our corporate retreat packages start at a minimum of 15 persons, with rates beginning at ₱4,500 per person for day use and ₱6,500 per person for overnight stays. This includes exclusive use of our facilities, team building equipment, a dedicated events coordinator, meals (breakfast, lunch, and dinner for overnight stays), and use of our conference rooms. For groups larger than 50 persons, we offer special bulk rates. Our maximum capacity is 120 persons for comfortable accommodation.
How far in advance should we book for a corporate event, and what's the reservation process?
We recommend booking at least 2 months in advance for corporate events, especially during peak seasons (October-December). To reserve, we require a 30% non-refundable deposit and signed booking agreement. The remaining balance is due 2 weeks before your event date. The reservation process starts with an initial consultation (in-person or virtual) to discuss your specific needs, followed by a customized proposal within 48 hours. We can hold your preferred date for 3 working days while you make your decision.
What happens if it rains during our outdoor team building activities?
We have comprehensive wet weather contingency plans in place. Our 500-square-meter covered pavilion and indoor activity areas can accommodate all our team building programs. We also maintain flexible scheduling - if rain is forecast, we can adjust the program timing or switch to our indoor alternatives without compromising the team building experience. All bookings include both outdoor and indoor backup options at no additional cost.
How do you handle special dietary requirements for corporate groups?
Our in-house catering team can accommodate various dietary requirements including vegetarian, vegan, halal, and food allergies. Please inform us of any special dietary needs at least 1 week before your event. We provide detailed menu options during the planning phase and can customize meals accordingly. For groups with multiple dietary restrictions, we recommend scheduling a menu tasting session (available for groups of 50 or more) two weeks before the event.
Can we bring our own team building facilitator or do we need to use yours?
While you're welcome to bring your own facilitator, our packages include certified team building professionals who are familiar with our facilities and equipment. If you choose to bring your own facilitator, we offer a facility-only rate with a 10% discount on the standard package price. However, they must attend a brief orientation session with our staff at least one day before the event and sign our safety waiver. We also require proof of their professional liability insurance.
