Frequently Asked Questions About Family Staycation Packages

What are your current room rates and staycation packages?
Our luxury villa rates start at ₱15,000 per night for our Deluxe Pool Villa (good for 6 pax) and ₱25,000 for our Grand Villa (good for 12 pax). All packages include exclusive pool access, complimentary breakfast, use of karaoke facilities, and free parking. For larger groups, our Premium Villa (up to 20 pax) is available at ₱35,000 per night. Additional guests are charged ₱1,000 per head. We also offer special weekday rates and seasonal promotions - please contact us for current offers.
How far are you from San Fernando, Pampanga and what's the best way to get there?
We're located in Bacolor, approximately 15 minutes (8 km) from San Fernando City proper. From San Fernando, take MacArthur Highway and turn right at Bacolor-Sta. Barbara Road. We're easily accessible by private car with free secure parking on-site. For guests coming from Manila, we're about 1.5 hours away via NLEX. We can also arrange private van transfers from Clark International Airport or assist with directions for commuting options.
Do you allow early check-in or late check-out?
Standard check-in time is 2:00 PM and check-out is at 12:00 PM. Early check-in from 11:00 AM and late check-out until 2:00 PM may be arranged subject to availability, with additional fees starting at ₱2,000. Please inform us in advance if you need flexible check-in/out times so we can check availability and make necessary arrangements.
What amenities are included in the villa and what should we bring?
Each villa comes fully equipped with air conditioning, LED TV with Netflix, complete kitchen facilities (refrigerator, microwave, rice cooker, utensils), hot and cold shower, and fresh towels/linens. We provide basic toiletries, drinking water, and initial coffee/tea supplies. For swimming, please bring your own towels and swimwear. For cooking, you'll need to bring your own ingredients, though we can arrange catering services with advance notice. We also have an in-house mini grocery for basic needs.
Can we hold events like birthdays or family reunions at the resort?
Yes, we welcome special events! Our villas can accommodate intimate gatherings, with our events package including basic setup, use of sound system, and event coordination. Event fees start at ₱5,000 on top of villa rates, depending on guest count and requirements. We require advance booking of at least 2 weeks for events. Maximum capacity varies by villa: 30 pax for Deluxe, 50 pax for Grand, and 80 pax for Premium. We can also recommend trusted suppliers for catering, decoration, and photography services.
