Frequently Asked Questions About Day Tour and Resort Access

What are your day tour rates and what's included?
Our day tour rates start at ₱1,500 per person on weekdays and ₱2,000 on weekends/holidays. This includes full access to our swimming pools, jacuzzi, cabanas, and sports facilities from 8AM to 5PM. We also provide complimentary welcome drinks, use of basic amenities like towels and loungers, and free parking. For children below 4 feet in height, the rate is ₱800. Note that food and beverages from our restaurant are not included in the day tour rate.
How do I book a day tour and what's the payment process?
You can book through our website or by calling us at +63 945 123 4567. We require a 50% deposit to secure your reservation, payable via bank transfer (BDO or BPI) or GCash. The remaining balance should be paid upon arrival. For large groups of 15 or more, please book at least 1 week in advance. We'll send you a confirmation email with your booking details and receipt once payment is received.
How far are you from Clark Airport and can you arrange transportation?
We're located approximately 15 minutes from Clark International Airport in Dau, Pampanga. While we don't provide direct shuttle service, we can arrange private van transfers for ₱1,200 one-way (up to 6 passengers). Alternatively, Grab cars are readily available from the airport, typically costing ₱350-500. Just provide your booking reference when requesting directions.
What's your rescheduling and cancellation policy?
We allow one free rebooking if requested at least 72 hours before your scheduled visit. For cancellations made 5 days or more in advance, we provide a full refund minus a 10% processing fee. Cancellations within 3-4 days receive a 50% refund. Unfortunately, we cannot offer refunds for cancellations made less than 48 hours before the booking date, but we can offer a rebooking within 30 days for a ₱500 fee per person.
Can we bring our own food and drinks to the resort?
Outside food and beverages are not allowed inside the resort as we have our own restaurant and bar serving local and international cuisine. However, we make exceptions for special dietary requirements (please inform us in advance) and birthday cakes with a minimal corkage fee of ₱300. We also offer pre-ordered meal packages starting at ₱850 per person which include lunch and afternoon snacks.
