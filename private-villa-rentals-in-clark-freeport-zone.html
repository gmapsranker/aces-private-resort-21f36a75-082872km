Frequently Asked Questions About Private Villa Rentals

What's included in the private villa rental rate at Aces Resort?
Our all-inclusive villa rates cover your private pool access, fully-furnished accommodation, complete kitchen facilities, free WiFi, and parking for 2 vehicles. Each villa includes air conditioning throughout, LED TV with cable, and a BBQ grilling area. We also provide 24/7 security, daily housekeeping, and complete bedding with fresh linens. Additional amenities like in-villa massage services and private chef arrangements are available for an extra fee.
How far is Aces Resort from Clark International Airport?
Aces Private Resort is conveniently located just 10 minutes (approximately 5 kilometers) from Clark International Airport within Clark Freeport Zone. We offer airport pickup and drop-off services for an additional fee of P800 per way. Many guests find our location ideal as it's also just 15 minutes from SM City Clark and major Clark attractions while maintaining a private, secluded atmosphere.
What is your booking and cancellation policy?
We require a 50% deposit to secure your reservation, payable via bank transfer or credit card. The remaining balance is due upon check-in. For cancellations made at least 7 days before check-in, we offer a full refund minus a P2,000 processing fee. Cancellations within 7 days of check-in will result in a 50% charge of the total booking amount. No-shows will be charged in full.
Can we bring our own food and drinks to the villa?
Yes, you're welcome to bring your own food and beverages. Each villa has a fully-equipped kitchen including a refrigerator, microwave, rice cooker, and basic cooking utensils. We also have a BBQ area you can use. For convenience, we offer grocery delivery service from nearby stores for a small fee. Please note that while outside food is allowed, we require proper food waste disposal to maintain cleanliness.
What's your maximum capacity per villa and do you allow day tours?
Our villas have different capacities: Standard villas accommodate up to 8 guests, while our Premium villas can host up to 12 guests. We charge P1,000 per additional person beyond the base capacity, up to a maximum of 4 extra guests per villa. For day tours, we offer 8-hour access from 8AM to 4PM at special rates, subject to availability. All day tour guests must register and follow our resort policies.
