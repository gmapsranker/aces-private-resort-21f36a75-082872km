Frequently Asked Questions About Wellness and Spa Services

What are your current room rates and what's included in the stay?
Our deluxe rooms start at ₱5,500 per night for two persons, while our premium suites range from ₱7,500-12,000 per night. All bookings include complimentary breakfast, access to our infinity pool, fitness center, and meditation garden. Weekend and holiday rates may be slightly higher. For the full resort experience, we recommend our wellness package at ₱15,000 which includes a 2-night stay, daily spa treatments, yoga sessions, and healthy meal plans customized by our nutritionist.
How far in advance should I book for weekend stays or special occasions?
For weekend stays, we recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance as we often reach full capacity, especially during peak season (December-May). For special occasions or group bookings of 5 rooms or more, please book at least 1 month ahead. During local festivals in Pampanga like the Hot Air Balloon Festival, we suggest booking 2-3 months in advance. We require a 50% deposit to secure your reservation.
What spa treatments do you offer and how long do they take?
Our spa offers traditional Filipino hilot massage (60/90 mins, ₱2,000/₱2,800), aromatherapy massage (60 mins, ₱2,500), hot stone therapy (90 mins, ₱3,000), and facial treatments using local organic ingredients (60 mins, ₱1,800). We also offer couples' packages in our private spa suites. All treatments begin with a foot ritual and end with ginger tea service. We recommend booking spa services at least 24 hours in advance.
Is there transportation available from Clark International Airport to the resort?
Yes, we provide private airport transfers from Clark International Airport for ₱1,500 per vehicle (up to 4 passengers). The journey takes approximately 45 minutes. Please provide your flight details at least 48 hours before arrival so we can arrange your pickup. We also offer day tours to local attractions like Mt. Pinatubo and heritage sites in partnership with accredited local tour operators.
What is your cancellation and refund policy?
For room bookings, we offer full refunds for cancellations made at least 7 days before check-in. Cancellations within 3-6 days of check-in will receive a 50% refund. For cancellations less than 72 hours before check-in, the deposit becomes non-refundable. For spa services, we require 24 hours notice for cancellation to avoid a 50% charge. During peak seasons and special events, special cancellation policies may apply which will be communicated during booking.
